# Doc: Definition of User Stories


### Core Stories:
* **Parent:** "As a Parent, I want to view available slots for teachers and studios so I can easily book a coaching session for my child."
* **Teacher:** "As a Teacher, I want to set my availability and confirm lesson completion so that my payroll and studio usage are tracked correctly."
* **Admin:** "As an Admin, I want to validate coaching requests within 48h to ensure no scheduling conflicts occur."
* **Community:** "As a member of the school, I want to list/rent costumes and props to reuse existing materials and save costs."


## US-01: Create Coaching Initiative
**As a** Teacher  
**I want to** create a coaching initiative with dates, studio, capacity, and cost per session  
**So that** I can submit it for Management approval and offer structured coaching sessions to students  


## US-02: Approve Coaching Session
**As Management**  
**I want to** approve or reject a submitted coaching session request.  
**So that** only validated coaching sessions become available to the student.  

---

## US-03: Schedule Coaching Session
**As a** Student  
**I want to** request a coaching session.
**So that** I can participate in additional training while respecting availability constraints 

---

## US-04: Confirm Session Completion
**As a** Teacher or Student  
**I want to** confirm that a coaching session has taken place  
**So that** the session can be validated and processed financially by Management

---

## US-05: Access Digital Inventory
**As a** Student  
**I want to** browse and rent items from the digital inventory  
**So that** I can access the resources required for classes or performances

---

## US-06: Add Item to Inventory
**As** Management
 **I want to** add school-owned costumes and accessories to the official Digital Inventory
**So that** they can be rented by the school community for a symbolic fee.

---

## US-07: View Studio Availability
**As a** Teacher  
**I want to** view studio availability for specific dates and times  
**So that** I can plan or request coaching sessions accordingly 

---

## US-08: Manage Teacher Availability
**As a** Teacher  
**I want to** define and manage my coaching availability  
**So that** students can only book sessions when I am available 
 
---

## US-09: Receive Notifications
**As a** Student, Teacher, or Management  
**I want to** receive notifications regarding coaching and inventory updates  
**So that** I remain informed about relevant system activities  

---

## US-10: Cancel Coaching Session
**As a** Student  
**I want to** cancel a scheduled coaching session with a justified reason  
**So that** the system can apply the appropriate attendance and financial rules 

---

## US-11: View Participation History
**As a** Student or Teacher  
**I want to** view my coaching participation history  
**So that** I can track past and upcoming sessions  

---

## US-12: Generate Financial Summary
**As Management**  
**I want to** generate a financial summary based on validated coaching sessions  
**So that** accounting processes can be executed accurately 

---

## US-13: Validate Session Completion
**As Management**  
**I want to** validate confirmed coaching sessions  
**So that** only officially validated sessions are included in reports and billing  

---

## US-14: Ensure Studio Optimization
**As Management**  
**I want to** monitor and optimise studio usage  
**So that** school resources are efficiently allocated  

---

## US-15: Request to Join Coaching Session
**As a** Student
**I want to** request to join an existing coaching session that still has available capacity
**So that** I can participate in the class alongside the original creator without exceeding the format limit.

---

## US-16: Validate Session Adhesion Request
**As a** Teacher / Management
**I want to** approve or reject a student's request to join an existing coaching session based on available capacity and pedagogical fit
**So that** only validated students are allowed to attend the class without exceeding the room or format limits.

---

## US-17: Add Item to Community Marketplace
**As a** Teacher or Student
**I want to** list an item on the Community Marketplace
**So that** I can sell, rent, or gift it to other members of the school.
