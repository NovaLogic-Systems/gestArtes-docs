# Business Rules

## 1. Accounts & Access

- BR-01: Each student has a single system account.
- BR-02: A Student account may be operated by a Parent/Guardian.
- BR-03: The system does not differentiate between Student and Parent usage.
- BR-04: Access to the platform requires authentication (login required).

## 2. Studio & Scheduling Constraints

- BR-05: Not all class types are compatible with all studios.
- BR-06: A studio cannot be double-booked.
- BR-07: Coaching sessions may occur outside standard school hours, with adjusted pricing.
- BR-08: Coaching sessions may be scheduled at short notice.
- BR-09: Both studio and teacher availability must be validated before final approval.

## 3. Coaching Requests & Approval

- BR-10: Coaching sessions require validation by Management.
- BR-11: Coaching requests may be initiated by Student or Teacher.
- BR-12: A coaching session is only considered confirmed after Management approval.
- BR-13: A dual validation process is required after the session:
  - Teacher or Student confirms completion.
  - Management validates finalisation.
- BR-14: Coaching request validation must occur within 48 hours.

## 4. Attendance & Cancellation

- BR-15: If a student misses a session without prior notice, the session is charged.
- BR-16: Cancellation requires a justification to be provided.
- BR-17: Special pricing applies to external sessions or sessions outside standard hours.

## 5. Financial & Reporting

- BR-18: The base coaching price is 36€/hour, subject to contextual variation.
- BR-19: After validation, session data is automatically included in a financial summary table.
- BR-20: The system generates summary tables for accounting purposes but does not perform direct invoicing.

## 6. Digital Inventory

- BR-21: The digital inventory is exclusive to the internal school community.
- BR-22: The default transaction type for items is rental.
- BR-23: Students and Teachers may add items to the School Marketplace.
- BR-24: School-owned items may only be rented and cannot be sold.
- BR-25: When renting school-owned items, condition and return date must be verified.
- BR-26: A symbolic fee may apply to internal item usage.

## 7. Operational Constraints

- BR-27: Studio occupancy should be optimized to avoid unnecessary idle time.
- BR-28: Teachers must explicitly define their availability in the system.
- BR-29: Certain modalities may involve more than one teacher in a single session.
